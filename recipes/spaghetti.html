Spaghetti recipe

Description

The Allrecipes community adores this lasagna.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato products
  • Sugar
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
